Hello, I'm Derek

If that wasn't clear already haha.

I am a student currently attending Brigham Young University. I began college as an electrical engineering student but am now pursuing a path in Information Systems.

I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Fiji for two years and returned April of 2020, when many missionaries were sent home because of the COVID-19 virus.

An Unexpected Challenge

As someone who was recently diagnosed with an irreversible muscular dystrophy, I have been truly grateful for the opportunities I have been given, such as growing my physical talent in tumbling and tricking.

I still wait for the day when the researchers in FSHD find a cure and are able to stop my muscles from deteriorating, but until then I will try to live life to the fullest.
